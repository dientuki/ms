    Mobility + Security

    The mobile productivity your people want, the data protection your company needs.

    It’s one of today’s most important business challenges: how to empower users to be productive wherever they are, while protecting the massive amounts of data moving through your mobile environment? Microsoft Enterprise Mobility + Security (EMS) provides the tools to achieve both – for maximum mobile productivity.

    01

    Keep people productive from virtually anywhere, on any device

    01. Keep people productive from virtually anywhere, on any device

    Empower your users to do great work using the devices and apps they love, by delivering mobile experiences that work the way they should, right from the start

    • Single sign-on
    • Device positioning
    • Auto updates
    • Microsoft Office apps
    • Self-service
    Freedom to work from any device
    With EMS, people can work securely from virtually any phone, tablet or PC – whether it’s corporate-owned, employee-owned, or a third-party managed device (such as a kiosk or contractor-supplied device).
    Productivity right from the start
    EMS allows you to configure a device with policies and certificates so users can easily access email, Wi-Fi, apps, and other corporate resources – ensuring that devices are productivity-ready with minimal user setup.
    Streamlined access to all apps
    Provide the convenience of single sign-on access to thousands of cloud and on-premises applications with one unified identity. This includes Microsoft Office apps as well as support for Salesforce, Concur, Workday, and thousands of other popular SaaS apps.
    The Office experiences people expect
    Give your people the familiarity of the real Microsoft Office experience, while maintaining unparalleled control over the data that moves through Outlook, Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and Teams.
    Empower your users with self-service capabilities
    With EMS, users can perform a variety of tasks without having to contact IT or helpdesk. Self-service options include password/PIN reset, as well as the ability to join and manage groups. Users can also get access to apps and invite external partners to collaborate within corporate apps.

    02

    Protect your data at every turn

    02. Protect your data at every turn

    With EMS, only the right people, under the right conditions, can access your company data. What’s more, apps and data stay protected – even after they’ve been accessed.

    • App encryption at rest
    • App access control
    • Save as/copy/paste restrictions
    • App-level selective wipe
    • Managed web browsing
    • Windows 10 information protection
    • Secure viewing of PDFs, images, videos
    • Restrict features, sharing, downloads
    Enable users to share files securely
    Allow protecting and sharing sensitive data with people inside and outside of your organization. You can also control and track what happens after a file has been accessed, with the ability to prevent copying, printing, and other actions that help control sharing with unauthorized users.
    Control access to data
    based on real-time context
    With conditional access, you can define policies that control access to your corporate data based on a variety of conditions, including user, location, device state, application sensitivity, and real-time risk. As conditions change, controls kick in to ensure that your data is protected with the ability to challenge users with multi-factor authentication, block access, provide access with limited functionality, prompt for device enrollment, or even wipe data from the device.
    Protect apps with comprehensive policies
    Office Mobile and other apps can be protected with a rich set of app protection policies, like the ability to restrict copy/paste and save-as. This protects apps and data both before and after access. With EMS, you can protect data at the app level without the need for device enrollment.

    03

    Empower your IT team

    03. Empower your IT team

    Give your administrators the tools and support to manage your diverse mobile ecosystem more strategically and proactively.

    • Company Managed
      • - Information worker / Shared
    • Employee Managed
      • - Primary / Companion
    • Third-Party Managed
      • - Public Work / Contractor

    Tackle mobile complexity with flexible management options
    EMS allows you to protect at the user, device, app, and file levels, giving you an incredible amount of flexibility and choice in how you solve for a variety of needs within your organization. You even have the option to protect corporate data on unmanaged devices, enabling data protection across a wider range of scenarios.
    Manage everything from one unified, modern console
    Our Azure-based portal gives you a unified EMS administrative experience that allows for streamlined management of core EMS workflows across Azure AD and Intune. You can also manage devices, users, and groups with nearly unlimited scale.
    Simplify user lifecycle management
    With a single identity across all IT functions, integration with HR applications, and the use of dynamic groups, you can simplify provisioning of users across application during every phase of their lifecycle in the organization. Manage who has access to what when employees join or leave the company or when they move across teams.
    Simplify, automate, and integrate workloads
    Microsoft Graph API connects developers to the data that drives productivity – mail, calendar, contacts, documents, directory, devices, and more. EMS gives you the capability to connect different services and automate workflows and processes between them.
    Deployment planning and ongoing support set you up for success
    FastTrack provides access to a team of engineers dedicated to helping you plan and execute your EMS deployment. And our 24/7 global support keeps you running strong day to day. Both services are included with your EMS subscription.
    Cloud architecture that just works
    As a cloud solution that integrates with your on-premises infrastructure, EMS takes the worry out of maintenance and updates. You get the confidence of knowing that your system is always up to date and ready to scale globally as your needs change.
